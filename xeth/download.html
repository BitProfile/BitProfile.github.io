A new community-created blockchain will be available for activation at block #1920000, which is expected to arrive on July 20th 2016, in which the Ether balance of contract 0xbb9bc244d798123fde783fcc1c72d3bb8c189413 (commonly referred to as "The DAO") and all of its descendants will be moved to a withdraw contract due to an exploited bug in the original contract. This should restore funds from all contracts linked to the exploit and allow authenticated holders of "The DAO" tokens to redeem it for ether.

Xeth supports the DAO hard fork by default.
If you don't want to support the hard fork, please start Xeth with the --dao-fork=0 command line argument.
